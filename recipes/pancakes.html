Pancakes

Simple but tasty pancakes

Ingredients

  • 750ml milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 400g flour
  • 1 shot soda

Steps

  1. Mix everything except the soda together until you get a smooth dough.
  2. Put the dough for half an hour in the fridge.
  3. After that add the soda shot into the dough and heat your stove.
  4. The pan should be evenly heated before you fry the pancakes.
  5. Bon appétit.
