Forku Kebab

Homemade kebab with veggi chicken, tofu and vegetables in soy sauce

Ingredients

  • pita bread
  • veggie chicken
  • tofu
  • zucchini
  • pepper
  • arugula
  • shepherd's cheese
  • cayenne pepper
  • soy sauce
  • rosemary(spice)
  • parsley(fresh or spice)

Steps

  1. At first you wash and cut the vegetables, then cook them in a pan
  2. fry the veggie chicken in a pan, add spices to your liking
  3. heat up the oven, put the pita bread in it when ready
  4. wash the arugula and put it in a bowl
  5. cut the shepherd's cheese and also put it in a bowl
  6. when the vegetables are cooked add soy sauce, cayenne pepper, rosemary and parsley
  7. when ready put the pita breda out of the oven
  8. everything's ready now so you can start filling the pita bread with vegetables, chicken, arugula and cheese
  9. that's it, bon appétit.
