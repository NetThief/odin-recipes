Tofu Rice

Rice, fresh vegetables and tofu bring together a fresh and healthy combination for your dinner.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tofu, smoked if u like
  • Zucchini
  • Pepper
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Any vegetables you like to add

Steps

  1. Put the rice in a pot, add water and let it cook
  2. Cut the zucchini, pepper and tofu
  3. Add oil in a pan, put the vegetables in it
  4. Let them cook for a few minutes
  5. When the rice is ready put it in the pan
  6. Add soy sauce and sesame oil
  7. Then add salt and pepper, also other spices you like. I like to add some parsley and cayenne pepper
  8. Bon appétit.
